# Your Company is a Filesystem

**Author:** Eli Mernit (@mernit)
**Date:** February 10, 2026
**Source:** https://x.com/mernit/status/2021324284875153544

## Key Takeaways

- The most effective AI agent architecture reduces to two components: the filesystem as state and an LLM as the orchestrator.
- Modeling a company's operations as a filesystem allows agents to solve business problems through simple file reads and writes, eliminating the need for complex APIs or databases.
- Openclaw's effectiveness stems from grounding its entire context in the local filesystem, making the agent's state transparent and inspectable.
- The filesystem serves as the single source of truth for agents, which is what makes them practically useful rather than theoretical.
- Communication channels like Telegram or iMessage become thin interfaces over file-backed agent state, keeping the architecture simple and composable.

## One-Line Summary

The most powerful AI agent architecture treats your entire company as a filesystem, reducing all business logic to reading and writing files orchestrated by an LLM.
