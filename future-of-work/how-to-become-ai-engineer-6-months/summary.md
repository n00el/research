# How to Become an AI Engineer in 6 Months (RESOURCES)

**Author:** Ronin (@DeRonin_)
**Date:** 2026-03-16
**Source:** https://x.com/DeRonin_/status/2033587293064204349

## Key Takeaways

- Month 1 focuses on Python, Git, CLI, APIs, SQL, Pandas, and FastAPI as foundational coding skills before touching any AI
- Month 2 covers LLM app development: prompting, structured outputs, function calling, streaming, conversation state, cost optimization, and prompt injection defense
- Month 3 is dedicated entirely to RAG: embeddings, chunking strategies, vector databases, metadata filtering, reranking, retrieval quality metrics, and hallucination reduction
- Month 4 introduces agents (ReAct, plan-and-execute), multi-tool orchestration, workflow design, and evaluation frameworks (LLM-as-judge, automated testing)
- Months 5-6 are about building portfolio projects, Docker deployment, cloud infrastructure, LLM observability, and interview prep
- The roadmap prioritizes practical depth over breadth, treating each month as a prerequisite for the next

## One-Line Summary

A structured 6-month curriculum for becoming an AI engineer, progressing from Python fundamentals through LLM apps, RAG systems, and agent architectures to production deployment.
