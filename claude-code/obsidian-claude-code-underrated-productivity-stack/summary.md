# Obsidian + Claude Code is the most underrated productivity stack in tech right now

**Author:** CyrilXBT (@cyrilXBT)
**Date:** Mar 22, 2026
**Source:** https://x.com/cyrilXBT/status/2035761611764347219

## Key Takeaways

- Obsidian's plain-text markdown files make your entire knowledge base readable by any AI -- no cloud lock-in, no proprietary formats.
- Claude Code running locally against your Obsidian vault transforms it from static notes into a contextual AI assistant that answers from YOUR research, not the internet.
- The real power goes beyond retrieval: Claude Code can find research gaps, connect ideas across months of notes, draft content in your voice, and build custom dashboards on your vault.
- Most people use AI as a search engine replacement; the next level is using it as an extension of your own accumulated thinking and pattern recognition.
- For crypto/AI builders and content creators, this stack turns months of captured research into an unfair information advantage.

## One-Line Summary

Obsidian as a local markdown knowledge base combined with Claude Code's file-access capabilities creates a personal JARVIS that reasons over your own accumulated research and thinking rather than generic internet knowledge.
