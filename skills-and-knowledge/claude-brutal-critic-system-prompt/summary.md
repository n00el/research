# How to Turn Claude Into Your Brutal Critic

**Author:** Ruben Hassid (@rubenhassid)
**Date:** March 13, 2026
**Source:** https://x.com/rubenhassid/status/2032382456704201108

## Key Takeaways

- A 4-step system prompt framework to make Claude stop being a yes-man and instead act as a brutally honest thinking partner
- Step 1: Force Claude to distinguish what you're actually saying from what you think you're saying -- surface self-deception
- Step 2: Make Claude dissect your reasoning like a mechanic, showing exactly WHY your logic is flawed and what assumptions it rests on
- Step 3: Have Claude calculate the real cost of your avoidance behaviors -- procrastination, waiting for the "right time", comfortable stories
- Step 4: Ask Claude to show the concrete gap between your current approach and what someone who's already succeeded would do differently
- The post references a longer article ("How to set up Claude the right way") as a companion guide for full Claude configuration

## One-Line Summary

A 4-step system prompt that transforms Claude from an agreeable assistant into a brutally honest thinking partner that challenges your reasoning, exposes self-deception, and quantifies the cost of avoidance.
