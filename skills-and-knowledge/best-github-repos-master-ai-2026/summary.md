# Best GitHub Repos to Master AI From Scratch in 2026

**Author:** Nav Toor (@heynavtoor)
**Date:** March 15, 2026
**Source:** https://x.com/heynavtoor/status/2033240431790797239

## Key Takeaways

- Ten curated open-source GitHub repos covering the full AI learning journey from beginner to advanced, all free and continuously maintained by Microsoft, Hugging Face, Stanford researchers, and independent engineers.
- Microsoft's "Generative AI for Beginners" (21 lessons) and "AI Agents for Beginners" (12 lessons) are recommended as the two most essential repos for foundational and career-relevant skills.
- Sebastian Raschka's "LLMs from Scratch" stands out for hands-on understanding of transformer internals by coding a GPT-style model in PyTorch from the ground up.
- RAG and AI Agents are identified as the two highest-demand, lowest-supply skills in the 2026 job market, with ManpowerGroup's survey citing AI skills as the #1 hardest to find worldwide.
- The article provides three structured learning paths: complete beginner (6-week plan), developer deepening understanding, and career-optimized path focusing on agents and RAG.
- DAIR.AI's Prompt Engineering Guide is highlighted as the definitive prompting resource, cited by both Google and OpenAI.
- The core thesis is that free open-source education is abundant but the competitive advantage comes from actually completing the material, not just bookmarking it.

## One-Line Summary

A curated list of ten free, open-source GitHub repositories that form a complete curriculum for learning AI from scratch in 2026, with structured learning paths for different skill levels.
