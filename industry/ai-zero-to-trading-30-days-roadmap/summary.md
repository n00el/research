# How I'd Use AI to Go From Zero to Trading in 30 Days

**Author:** darkzodchi (@zodchiii)
**Date:** 2026-03-16
**Source:** https://x.com/zodchiii/status/2033478855562772608

## Key Takeaways

- Week 1 focuses on learning fundamentals (Expected Value, Kelly Criterion, base rates) using Claude as a tutor -- no trades until you can explain the math
- Week 2 builds a research system: analyze live contracts with AI, practice calibration by documenting predictions and checking accuracy after 3 days
- Week 3 starts with embarrassingly small trades ($20-50) to experience real skin-in-the-game emotions; 86% of worst trades correlated with being tilted or bored
- Week 4 converts ad-hoc trading into a rule-based system with explicit ENTRY, SIZING, EXIT, and TILT rules -- "if a rule has the word 'feel' in it, replace it with a number"
- Three AI prompts that matter most: "Am I missing something?" (steelman opposition), "Calibrate me" (check overconfidence), "Post-mortem" (separate process from luck)
- The first month isn't about profit -- it's about building the machine (system + psychology + AI workflow) that eventually generates returns

## One-Line Summary

A 30-day roadmap using Claude as a trading tutor and research system, progressing from probability fundamentals through calibration practice to building a disciplined, rule-based trading system.
