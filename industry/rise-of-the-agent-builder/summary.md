# The Rise of the Agent Builder

**Author:** Zach Lloyd (@zachlloydtweets) — Founder & CEO of Warp
**Date:** March 4, 2026
**Source:** https://x.com/zachlloydtweets/status/2029223875066687683

## Key Takeaways

- Companies need a dedicated "Agent Builder" role to systematically replace SaaS tools with custom AI agents
- Coding agents generalize surprisingly well into general-purpose automation agents
- Warp is actively cancelling SaaS subscriptions in favor of agents built on their Oz platform
- The cultural shift: from "start with a prompt" to "can this be an ongoing agent?"
- Prediction: every 50+ person company will have a full-time agent builder within a year

## One-Line Summary

Warp's CEO argues that the "Agent Builder" — a dedicated role for replacing SaaS and manual workflows with AI agents — will become universal at companies over 50 people within a year.
