## Key Takeaways

- The awesome-chatgpt-prompts GitHub repository (143K+ stars, #33 most popular repo) is the largest open-source prompt library, working with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other LLMs
- The core principle is role-based prompting: telling AI "who to be" dramatically improves output quality compared to bare task descriptions
- A good prompt has 4 components: role, context, output format, and constraints -- most people only provide one (the task)
- The repo includes 150+ curated role-based prompts (Linux Terminal, Financial Analyst, Code Reviewer, Debate Partner, etc.) plus a 25-chapter prompt engineering course
- Specialized prompts for prediction market analysis (Polymarket) show how structured prompting turns AI from opinion generator into analytical tool

## One-Line Summary

The awesome-chatgpt-prompts repository demonstrates that role-based prompting with explicit context, format, and constraints transforms mediocre AI outputs into expert-level responses across any domain.
