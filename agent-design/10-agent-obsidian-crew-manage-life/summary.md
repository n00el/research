# PhD Student Built a 10-Agent Obsidian Crew to Manage His Entire Life

**Author:** Om Patel (@om_patel5)
**Date:** Mar 22, 2026
**Source:** https://x.com/om_patel5/status/2035533269429612663

## Key Takeaways

- A PhD student in AI built a multi-agent system within Obsidian to automate personal life management (papers, deadlines, meetings, emails, health)
- Existing Obsidian setups failed because they required manual maintenance -- the exact bottleneck the user was trying to eliminate
- The system uses 10 agents and is voice-driven: the user "just talks" and agents handle organization automatically
- The project is open source, allowing community contributions and extensions

## One-Line Summary

An AI PhD student open-sourced a 10-agent Obsidian system that replaces manual personal knowledge management with voice-driven automation.
